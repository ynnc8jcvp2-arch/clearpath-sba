## 🎤 Handling Common Questions

### Q: "How is this different from [competitor product]?"
**A:** "We're not trying to be an enterprise ERP. We're the surgical tool—focused, fast, modular. We integrate WITH your systems, not replace them. And we're transparent about cost and timeline."

### Q: "What happens to our existing workflows?"
**A:** "Nothing changes without your approval. Phase 1 is integration and customization. We run parallel (ClearPath alongside your current process) until you're confident. Then you switch over."

### Q: "How do we train our team?"
**A:** "Part of Phase 2 is training documentation and video walkthroughs. For a 5-person team, we typically run a 2-hour training session. The interface is intentionally simple—if it takes more than 15 minutes to explain, we redesigned it."

### Q: "What about security and compliance?"
**A:** "All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow HIPAA-equivalent security practices. Audit logs are available for compliance review. You can host on your infrastructure (Trisura cloud) or ours (AWS/Azure). Your choice."

### Q: "Can you handle our specific financial spreading methodology?"
**A:** "Absolutely. We've built SBA 13(g)(2) as a reference. During discovery, we'll map your exact rules and build them in. This is Phase 1 work—codifying your expertise into the system."

### Q: "What if we want to add other lending domains later?"
**A:** "Perfect. That's why we built it modular. Equipment finance, trade finance, working capital—each domain reuses the parser and core infrastructure. New domain = 6-8 weeks of customization, not 6 months of building from scratch."

### Q: "How long until this is production-ready?"
**A:** "Phase 1-2 is 8-10 weeks. That includes your customization, UAT with your team, and security hardening. We can do a soft launch with one team, expand after 30 days."

### Q: "What's included in ongoing support?"
**A:** "Bug fixes, security patches, performance optimization. If you want new features or metrics (like a third-party integration), that's a separate line item. You stay in control of the roadmap."
